Why Donate Blood at FPT University Quy Nhơn?

Blood donation at FPT University Quy Nhơn supports our community, helping students, lecturers, and staff in medical emergencies. Each donation takes just 10 minutes and can save up to 3 lives. Join our campus-wide initiative to promote health and solidarity!

  • Boost your health and energy.
  • Contribute to FPTU Quy Nhơn’s community spirit.
  • Receive recognition and rewards for your contribution.

Who Can Donate at FPTU Quy Nhơn?

Students

Aged 18-60, healthy, weighing at least 50kg, and not pregnant or recently ill.

Lecturers & Staff

Healthy adults, no chronic diseases, and meeting weight/health requirements.

General Guidelines

No tattoos/piercings in the last 6 months, no alcohol 24 hours before, and eat a light meal.
